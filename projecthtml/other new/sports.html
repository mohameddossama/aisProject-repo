Pele Sends Message of Support to Neymar After Brazil’s FIFA World Cup 2022 Exit

Brazilian soccer great Pele congratulated Neymar for matching his national team scoring record on Friday, and then commiserated with the player for his World Cup exit. "I saw you grow, I cheered for you every day and I can finally congratulate you for reaching my number of goals with Brazil. We both know that this is more than a figure ," the 82-year-old Pele wrote.
